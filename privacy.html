1.2 Information We Don't Collect

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Data Protection

3.1 Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access to user data is restricted to essential personnel only

3.2 Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • VIP Activation Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Purchase Records: Retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • Inactive Accounts: Data is anonymized after 2 years of inactivity

Our service operates on the Telegram platform. Your interaction with the bot is subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.

Rin Bot is intended for educational purposes and may be used by students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
